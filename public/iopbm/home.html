Meet Dr. Angie Sadeghi, MD

Board-certified gastroenterologist and internist with over two decades of experience, dedicated to evidence-based, patient-centered care that prioritizes long-term health and disease prevention.

As founder and CEO of IOPBM, Dr. Sadeghi integrates advanced diagnostics, medical nutrition therapy, and lifestyle interventions to address the root causes of chronic illness.

  • Board certification in Gastroenterology and Internal Medicine
  • Fellowship training at LAC+USC Medical Center and CIRM
  • Author of The Trifecta of Health
  • Over 22 years of clinical experience
