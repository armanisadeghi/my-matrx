### Simple Rules
✅ **DO:**
- Use Navy for titles and important text
- Use Steel Blue sparingly as an accent (not for large areas)
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep text Dark Gray (`#475573`) for easy reading

❌ **DON'T:**
- Don't use Steel Blue for body text (hard to read)
- Don't mix too many colors - stick to these 4-5
- Don't use light gray text on white backgrounds (hard to see)

### Need One More Color?

- Light Blue: `#edf3fc` with Navy text

## Usage Guidelines

### Web/Digital

#### Hero Sections
- Background: Navy 950 (`#020817`)
- Text: White (`#ffffff`)
- Accents: Steel 400 (`#5b8fd9`)
- Secondary text: Slate 400 (`#8896ab`)

#### Body Content Sections
- Background: White (`#ffffff`) or Slate 50 (`#f4f6f9`)
- Headings: Navy 950 (`#020817`)
- Body text: Slate 600 (`#475573`) or Slate 800 (`#161b2e`)
- Links: Steel 400 (`#5b8fd9`)

#### Buttons

**Primary Button:**
```css
background: #ffffff;
color: #0a1628;
border: none;
```
On hover: `background: #d4e4fa`

**Secondary/Outline Button:**
```css
background: transparent;
color: #7daaeb;
border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.12);
```
On hover: `border-color: #5b8fd9; background: rgba(91,143,217,0.05)`

#### Interactive States
- Default link: Steel 400 (`#5b8fd9`)
- Hover: Steel 300 (`#7daaeb`)
- Active: Navy 600 (`#24406e`)
- Disabled: Slate 300 (`#b0bdd0`)

### Print Materials

#### Business Cards
- Front: Navy 900 background + White text + Steel 400 accent
- Back: White background + Navy 900 text

#### Brochures & Flyers
- Background: White or Slate 50
- Headlines: Navy 900
- Body text: Slate 700 or Slate 800
- Accents: Steel 400
- Callout boxes: Steel 50 background with Navy 600 text

#### Letterhead
- Header: Navy 900 or White (depending on background)
- Body: Slate 800
- Accent line: Steel 400

## Accessibility Notes

### Text Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1 AA)

**Passing Combinations:**
- White text on Navy 900+: ✅ Excellent contrast
- Navy 900 text on White: ✅ Excellent contrast
- Slate 600+ on White: ✅ Good for body text
- White text on Steel 400: ✅ Good for buttons
- Steel 400 on White: ✅ Good for links

**Avoid:**
- Slate 400 or lighter on White (insufficient contrast for body text)
- Steel 200 or lighter on White (insufficient contrast)
- Navy 500 text on Navy 900 background (too low contrast)
